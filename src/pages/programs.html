Programs

Tenda Wema has made significant strides in supporting vulnerable refugee and host communities in Kenya. Over the years, the organization has implemented various programs that directly impact the well-being and future of the people they serve. Below is an elaboration on the key programs undertaken by Tenda Wema.

Education

Education is a cornerstone of Tenda Wema's mission. The organization firmly believes in the transformative power of education as a tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. In 2023, Tenda Wema took a significant step by establishing Tenda Wema Community School in Kitengela, Kajiado, Kenya. This school serves around 300 pupils annually, providing them with quality education and an opportunity for a brighter future.

The Role of Tenda Wema Community School

The Tenda Wema Community School acts as a hub for positive change, nurturing socially responsible citizens and strengthening community bonds. By offering more than just academic knowledge, the school fosters a sense of civic duty and social responsibility among its students. The curriculum is designed to ensure students not only excel in academics but also grow into individuals who can contribute positively to society. Incorporating charity and compassion into the school’s ethos, Tenda Wema ensures that the students are well-rounded and aware of their role in their communities. The school serves as a model for holistic development, going beyond textbooks to instill values of empathy, leadership, and community service. This approach ensures that the graduates are not only academically sound but are also equipped to be future change makers

Educational Support Programs

To ensure that no child is left behind, Tenda Wema offers affordable fees rates (from as little as 3000ksh per term) to students from low-income families. This financial support allows vulnerable children to access quality education, an opportunity they might otherwise miss. The school also runs a school feeding program, which provides nutritious meals to students. This initiative addresses the issue of hunger, ensuring students remain focused and engaged in their studies. Tenda Wema also provides essential learning materials such as books and stationery. These resources help create a conducive learning environment and ensure that every child, regardless of their financial status, has the tools necessary to succeed in school.

French Language Proficiency

The Tenda Wema Community School stands out for its proficiency in the French language. Recognizing the importance of multilingualism in today’s world, the school offers French language classes as part of its curriculum. This not only benefits the refugee children, many of whom come from French-speaking countries, but also the children of the host community. The school extends French language tutoring services to the host community, providing an opportunity for children to learn a new language that can enhance their future prospects. By offering French language instruction, Tenda Wema bridges cultural and linguistic gaps, fostering greater integration and understanding within the community.

Accelerated Education Program

Tenda Wema also runs an Accelerated Education Program (AEP), which is specifically designed to help students who have fallen behind in their education due to displacement, conflict, or other challenges. This program admits students and offers them an intensive crash course, allowing them to catch up on missed schooling and proceed with their education at the appropriate level. The AEP works by condensing several years of education into a shorter timeframe, ensuring that students can quickly cover the material they need to advance. Teachers provide targeted instruction, focusing on core subjects to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to succeed. This program is particularly beneficial for older students who may have missed out on several years of schooling and need to re-enter the formal education system at a level appropriate for their age.

Livelihood

Tenda Wema Community Project plays a vital role in improving the livelihoods of vulnerable population by providing essential resources, such as food and clothing. These contributions directly impact the lives of refugee and host community members, ensuring they have access to basic needs like nutritious meals and proper clothing. By addressing these fundamental needs, Tenda Wema contributes to poverty alleviation and fosters an environment where families no longer have to worry about hunger or shelter. With basic necessities met, families can shift their focus to other critical aspects of their lives, such as pursuing education for their children or employment opportunities. The provision of food, clothing, and shelter acts as a safety net for vulnerable individuals, helping them regain stability and pursue economic independence. This initiative is crucial in promoting social welfare and preventing individuals from falling into deeper cycles of poverty.

Partnerships

Tenda Wema’s success is not only attributed to its internal programs but also to the strategic partnerships it has formed with key organizations. These partnerships are instrumental in supporting the organization's activities and expanding its reach.

Partnership with Inkomoko

Tenda Wema has a significant partnership with Inkomoko, an organization that specializes in providing business development services to refugees and host communities. Through this partnership, Tenda Wema has been able to access resources and expertise that enhance its socio-economic empowerment programs. Inkomoko supports Tenda Wema by offering training, mentorship, and access to financial opportunities/ investment support to better support the beneficiaries of Tenda Wema’s programs, helping them grow their and become financially independent.

Partnership with Cohere

Another partnership is with Cohere, an organization that works to support refugee-led organizations in delivering impactful services. Cohere offers capacity-building support to Tenda Wema, ensuring that the organization can effectively deliver its programs. This partnership has strengthened Tenda Wema’s ability to provide quality education, vocational training, and livelihoods support by equipping the organization with the necessary tools.

Partnership with RefugePoint

RefugePoint, an organization dedicated to providing solutions for refugees in need of lasting protection and opportunities, has also partnered with Tenda Wema. This collaboration focuses on expanding the reach of Tenda Wema’s programs, particularly in areas such as education and socio-economic empowerment.

Motivation

Tenda Wema Community Project continues to make a profound difference in the lives of vulnerable refugee and host communities through its comprehensive programs in livelihoods, education, and partnerships. The organization’s focus on providing essential resources, offering quality education, and forging strategic partnerships has created a lasting impact, helping individuals and families break free from cycles of poverty. Through its initiatives, Tenda Wema is not only changing lives today but also laying the foundation for a more empowered and self-sufficient community for the future.
